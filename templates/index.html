About IIC

Learn about our mission, functions, and the benefits we offer to the innovation ecosystem

Major Focus Areas

  • Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development
  • Incubation and Start-up Promotion
  • Industry Institute Interaction
  • Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)
  • Social Innovation

Functions of IIC

  • Promote innovation and entrepreneurship
  • Encourage creativity and problem-solving
  • Provide industry-academia interaction platform
  • Facilitate development of innovative ideas
  • Create culture of innovation and entrepreneurship

Benefits

  • Enhanced learning and teaching experience
  • Improved employability and entrepreneurship skills
  • Access to funding and incubation support
  • Collaboration with industry and academia
  • Recognition for innovative ideas and projects
